About

Formed in April 2013 by singer Dan Varley and guitarist Pete Long, West Yorkshire based Fie! Fie! Fie! have been entertaining audiences all over the country, with their alternative, electroacoustic sound.

More...

With Ethan Bowen on sax and keyboards, Zak Robinson on guitars and percussion, and John Feather on lead guitar, the band released their first, self produced album, The SILO SESSIONS, in November 2013. With such tracks as Time And Place, Shake It On Up, Classes and To The Water, the band went from strength to strength, playing festivals in Bristol, West Yorkshire and beyond, and building a growing reputation locally with many fantastic gigs.

Following the departure of John in July 2014, bassist and technical wizard, Avon Blyth, gave the band a much stronger and fuller sound, culminating in the release of the single, The Alternative, and the album CAN YOU HEAR THIS?, in early 2015. Songs like Papillon, Mantra, What Is It We Got? and North Landing Bay saw Fie! Fie! Fie! reach another level of popularity, with airplay across the world on stations like Blitz FM in Hollywood and BBC Introducing in the UK. Great festival spots at Saltaire in front of two thousand people, supporting Paul Heaton at Salford Festival, and many local and further-a-field gigs, made it hard to ignore what people called a 'busy', hard working band.

Releasing a new single, Hit The Spanish Main, in March 2016, and with plans to record a new album, Ethan and Zak went their separate ways - the age old reason of musical differences being the main issue. However, Dan, Pete and Avon, who were the core, and main songwriters of Fie!, weren't about to call it a day. Recruiting well respected local musician, Matt Burnside, on cajon, percussion, mandolin and other instruments, Fie! Fie! Fie! began the strongest phase of their career thus far.

Join us on our journey. See us live at festivals and gigs. Buy our music, and hear and read about us.
