Personal and confidence building guitar tuition in Heckmondwike, West Yorkshire

With 8 years' experience as a semi-professional musician, and a further 12 as a guitar teacher, P D Long Guitar Tuition can help you learn to play the guitar.

I work in Heckmondwike and across the West Yorkshire area. I have taught over 200 students to play the guitar, of all ages and to all levels. Some of my students have gone on to playing with bands and live on stage with a variety of musical acts. I consider the musician just as important as the music. As we work together to make you the legend you know you can be, your confidence will grow and grow. From the electric and bass to the classic acoustic guitar, my guitar lessons will help you learn everything you need to know.
